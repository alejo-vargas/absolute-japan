About Absolute Racing

Absolute Racingについて

Born On The Track.
Built For The Road.

Absolute Racing is one of Asia's most successful motorsport operations — and Absolute Japan is the natural next step.

Since 2010 we've competed and won across the highest levels of GT racing — GT World Challenge Asia, the 24 Hours of Le Mans, the Suzuka 10 Hours. From bases in Shanghai, Zhuhai, Bangkok and Sepang, we've earned trust handling some of the most demanding cars in the world.

Tokyo is our next chapter. The same precision and honesty that runs our race operations runs how we'll handle every car that comes through Absolute Japan.
